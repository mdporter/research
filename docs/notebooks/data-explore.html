1 Introduction

These plots show the relationship between the PHTS variable (on the x-axis) and the 1 year graft survival probability (y-axis) for a sample of patients. The horizontal dotted line is the overall survival probability. The bars show the 95% confidence intervals and any intervals colored red fall outside the overall surival (dotted line). The coloring is to help you quickly focus on variables that have some evidence of influence on survival.

The plots are in alphabetical order of the variable name.
